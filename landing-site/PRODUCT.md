# Al-Ansaar Landing

**Al-Ansaar** (الانصار) is the public landing page for a local-first readiness training app for the Shia community. It reframes Intizar — waiting for Imam al-Mahdi (atfs) — as active preparation across body, mind, and heart.

## Audience

Seekers discovering the app, community members looking for the Android release, and contributors finding the open-source home.

## Register

Solemn, empowering, nocturnal. Sacred Discipline — a high-performance spiritual instrument, not consumer wellness. Arabic calligraphy carries the emotional center (Dua Al-A'hd); English UI stays direct and spare.

## Promise

Arrive, feel the brand under a star-field with Imam al-Asr imagery, read the dua, understand the three pillars, download the APK from GitHub Releases.
